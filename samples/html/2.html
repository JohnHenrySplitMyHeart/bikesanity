Setting off - sunshine and happy trails

Date: Tuesday March 1, 2030

Distance: 29 km (17 miles)

Total distance: 29 km (17 miles)

The day began as I normally begin a bike tour: by getting in the saddle and riding. The weather was sunny and the birds were singing, and I realised that there was nothing else I'd rather be doing. I tried to take a photo of the birds while I was cycling along, but it didn't work so I continued on through along the happy trails through the verdant landscape.

The trails were indeed happy, and made for easy riding through this glorious landscape. This I knew I could photograph, and so I stopped and took a number of them, knowing this would make it easier for me to share this wonderful experience and make it easier to remember into the future.

What a view

Where did all those mountains come from?

Then I caught up with my buddies. Cycle touring can be better when you're solo, but it can be better with friends as well. It's good to try both, and to switch between them - and then to document your experience in a journal like this.

Riding with my buddies

This is really living the dream

Before I knew it I was back in the city. City riding can be more of a challenge than riding without traffic in the country, but can also be very worthwhile. Just keep your wits about you and make sure you enjoy it. And be careful when taking pictures.

I call this one "the urban jungle"

Before I knew it, I was home again. It always goes so quickly - and you'll be waiting for it all to start again. I couldn't believe how far I'd gone on just one day.
