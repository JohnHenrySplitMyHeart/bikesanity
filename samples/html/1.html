Background and introduction

Cycle touring in an excellent way to pass the time. Actually we all know that it's more than that - it's a way of life. My life was affirmed by cycle touring and yours will be too. You might think that I'm overselling it, but it's really true. Anyway, I enjoy cycle touring just a whole load and I'm going to tell you about it, in this admittedly invented sample journal.

This is the point in the journal where I'd probably tell you who I am and where I'm cycling. But of course I am just a figment, invented to create content for this simple sample journal. If you like, you can imagine me as the handsome fellow below. Though I'm not sure that's a very robust touring bike I have behind me.

Some people call me a hipster

It's also important to know where I'm going. Instead of describing this with place names, which can be confusing to look up if you're not familiar with an area, it's a really good idea to present this as a map. Of course, we want to use a standard mapping format that can be opened and shared anywhere, and there's none more standard than the GPX format. All your maps will be converted to GPX, and then displayed on the cool widget below.
